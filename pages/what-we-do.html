What We Do

Concerts

We will take all the worry and stress out of the entertainment end of your show. We will keep it on time and work with the band or bands management to give you the best show possible without killing your budget. Providing a turn key operation. Enables you to sit back and let us handle everything. We work very hard and take tremendous pride in what we do. We will go above and beyond to give you the best concert experience possible.

Corporate Events

Your event will be one everyone will remember. We do our best to incorporate your logo in everything we do so no matter what your guests are doing your logo will get there attention. We employ a professional staff to run your event and will blend in with your guests and staff. We will keep everything running smoothly and work beside your staff to exceed your expectations.

Festivals

Providing a smooth consistent flow whether it be one stage or multiple stages. We will work with you on scheduling so that there is always something going on. We will work our hardest to make the band change overs quick and smooth to keep your crowds attention and entertained. We are very aware that changes come up and we are fully prepared to accept those changes and keep the show rolling so your audience has an enjoyable experience. The show must go on!

Weddings

Do you want something different for your wedding? Give us your ideas and let us make them a reality! Let us make your special day one everyone remembers. We will take full scale event production and make it elegant and hidden to your guests. We will do our best to help take some of the stress out of planning your special day.

Church Events

We enjoy helping out churches and watching them grow. We understand your needs may be different from other faiths. We will meet those needs to your up most satisfaction. We work with your individual budget and give you by far the best production possible within that budget. Contact us and let's see what we can do to help you.

Training

We will work with you and your staff going over basics or more advanced techniques in audio, video, lighting, mixing techniques (gain structure), video production and lighting programing depending on your requirements. We come to your location and work with the equipment you already own so we can concentrate on problem areas within your location.

Installations

We will design the right system to fit your needs and budget at the an affordable price. We take your input and figure out what would work best for you. We are strong believers in that one system doesn't work for everybody. Each location and organization has different needs. We do our best to get those needs met the first time with a turnkey system. We can also design different phases of an upgrade so your system can grow as you grow.

Staging

We provide the area with staging rentals including full-service installation and take down. HB Stage Productions has experts in producing safe, stable, high-quality concert staging solutions. Our team will be dedicated to helping you produce the event you have envisioned. Whether you need a small stage for a town festival or a full arena-style concert stage for touring acts, you can trust HB Stage Productions to handle the project.
